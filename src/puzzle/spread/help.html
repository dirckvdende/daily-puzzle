Goal
Get the correct number of cells of each type after the simulation ends

Step 1: Place cells on the grid
Place some initial cells on the grid by selecting them at the bottom and then clicking/tapping cells in the grid.

Step 2: Simulate
Press the play button and watch the cells spread according to the arrows indicated. The simulation stops if nothing changes anymore.

Step 3: See the result
Now the number of cells of each type is added up and compared to the numbers below the selection buttons at the bottom. If they are the same, you win!

Note: During the simulation, a single spread step is performed row by row, from bottom to top and from left to right.
